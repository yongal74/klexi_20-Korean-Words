Frequently Asked Questions

Is Twenty Korean free?

Yes! Twenty Korean is completely free to use. All features including Hangeul learning, vocabulary flashcards, quizzes, TTS, and wrong answer review are available at no cost.

Do I need internet to use the app?

All vocabulary data is stored locally on your device. You can study flashcards and take quizzes offline. Text-to-speech requires an internet connection on some devices.

What TOPIK levels are covered?

Twenty Korean covers TOPIK I (Levels 1-2) and TOPIK II (Levels 3-4), with over 1,200 vocabulary words organized by topic and difficulty.

Can I learn Hangeul (Korean alphabet) from scratch?

Absolutely! The Hangeul section teaches all 14 basic consonants, 10 basic vowels, 5 double consonants, and 11 compound vowels with pronunciation guides and example words.

What are K-Drama expressions?

Each vocabulary word includes 5 K-Drama expressions - real phrases you'll hear in Korean dramas. These help you learn natural, conversational Korean that textbooks don't teach.

How does the wrong answer review work?

When you answer incorrectly in quizzes, those words are automatically saved to your review list with the number of times you've missed them. You can review them with TTS playback that reads the Korean word, English meaning, and example sentence.
